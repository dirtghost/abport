Cancer the Facts

I currently work as an ongoing editor and contributor for Cancer the Facts, an informational resource for patients. My writing is guided by my own experience as a former cancer patient having to navigate an incredibly complex and opaque subject during a life-changing event. My aim for each article is to not just reiterate findings from the latest oncology journals, but to provide readers a basic understanding of the science that informs this research. For me, having some grasp on the process guiding the medical decisions that could determine the fate of your health puts a little control back in the hands of patients.
